NorthStar Retail Group — Retail Analytics Project

I analyzed 3 years of transaction data for a simulated multi-region retailer to
answer one question: is this business growing sustainably? Using SQL, Python,
Excel, and Power BI, I found that while revenue grew a healthy ~12% year over
year for 3 straight years, that growth sits on some real risk — two regions
account for 42% of all revenue, one product category drives over half of sales,
and roughly 9 out of 10 new customers never come back after their first order.
Based on those findings, I put together a set of targeted recommendations,
including a customer retention offer projected to add real incremental revenue
if implemented.
